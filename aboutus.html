Our Story

My name is Joe and I created Furniture Dreams 15 years ago in the heart of Downtown Ontario and we have been serving the Inland Empire community ever since. I have been in the furniture business for at least 25 years, working with other stores and companies throughout Los Angeles county.

Our Purpose

We're here to help you. That's all we want to do. We want to find affordable furniture for you to give your home a new look and inspire you to choose different styles and explore other options. At Furniture Dreams, it's all about you.
